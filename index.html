Focaccia with Caramelized Onion,

cranberries,and Brie

wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with caramelized onion, tart, cramberries, and creamy pie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

imagine sally vegas

FEATURED IN:
fresh cranbarry recipes thatgo beyond souce

ingredients

for the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarn water
  • 1/4 cup of virgin olibe oil, plus more for the pan

for the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh of frozen cranberries

Method

1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients untill flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, thought i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last on the flour...
